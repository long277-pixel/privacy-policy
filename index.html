Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user information for the HeyCaddyAI mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Location Data: To provide accurate golf course distances and shot dispersion logic, our app requires access to your device's Precise Location. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on or transmitted to any external servers.

Bluetooth/Wear OS: The app uses Bluetooth to synchronize data with compatible Wear OS devices. This communication occurs strictly between your devices.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. All app logic, including the "Cone of Concern" calculations, is performed on-device.
